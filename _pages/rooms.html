When staying #InResidence at WestHouse Hotel New York, sumptuous comfort is heightened by the incomparable design and elegant style of the finest luxury bedrooms and suites in NYC. Evoking the glamour of a 1920's Carnegie Hall dressing room, well-framed luxury comes standard in all accommodations, with beveled mirrors and cultured images of Hollywood and Broadway hanging from Art Deco picture rails. Captivating views of the city’s quintessential rooftops, enjoyed through expansive windows, are simply unrivalled among any luxury hotels in NYC, as are views of Central Park and Times Square, adding an enviable enchantment to the WestHouse #InResidence experience. High ceilings crown gorgeous interior spaces with textured wall coverings, tufted leather headboards, director’s chairs, custom dark wood furnishings and plush flooring..

The pièce de résistance in select suites, elevating a stay at WestHouse Hotel New York to indulgent standards, are sumptuous Sleep | Studio beds boasting mattresses that marry the science of sleep with the beauty of design.
