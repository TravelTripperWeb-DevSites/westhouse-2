WestHouse Hotel New York is located in the heart of Midtown Manhattan, surrounded by some of the top things to do in NYC. In the midst of the city that never sleeps, the hotel is within minutes of the bright lights of Times Square, the unbeatable eats of Hell's Kitchen, the cultural institutions of the Upper West Side, and the lush green space of Central Park. Its address on 55th Street and 7th Avenue puts it near several city subway stations providing easy access to Manhattan activities near and far.
